FRESH LUMPIA

Fresh Lumpia, is a traditional Filipino dish made from fresh vegetables, meat, and shrimp wrapped in a soft lumpia wrapper.

It is different from fried lumpia because it is not deep-fried, making it lighter and healthier to eat.
